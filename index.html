Why do I do what I do?

I first discovered SEO when I was in high school and was a freelance ghost-writer focusing on writing tech and business articles. SEO interested me for both its marketing and technical aspects. Soon after I left the writing world and entered into the world of SEO where I started off working for small websites and was eventually contracted by a digital marketing and web design company where I performed SEO on law firm websites.
My path into learning development was an unexpected one. I stumbled upon a game that taught the user programming basics around ActionScript 3. From there I started to teach myself game development and app development using C#, Objective-C, Swift (version 1), and JavaScript. It wasn’t until about a year after I started learning development that I actually investigated, and fell in love with, web development.
